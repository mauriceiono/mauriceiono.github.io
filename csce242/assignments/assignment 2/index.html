About Ninjas

Topic Details Facts
Origin Japan Ninjas were created in the 14th century
Training Stealth and espionage Ninjas trained in espionage, sabotage, and assassinations
Tools Shuriken and kunai Shuriken were used to distract or harm enemies, while kunai were versatile tools and weapons
Clothing Black or dark clothing Ninjas wore dark clothing to blend into the night
