Iconic dragons

This is a website with cool looking dragons that you may or may not have seen before. I made this site becuase i always liked dragons and thought it would be cool to do this. For this project i decided to grab a picture of 2 dragons from video games you may or may not have heard of. The name of these dragons are fatalis and spyro.

The black dragon fatalis is from the game monster hunter and the apex of elders dragons in its world. In terms of age and power fatalis is said to be the strongest of its kind. The purple dragon Spryo the dragon is like the avatar of his world or chosen one. Spyro is a master of the elements like earth,ice,fire,electricity and even time. These dragons are both very powerful and not to be messed with.
